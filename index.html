How we work

Structured process.
No surprises mid-build.

Every engagement runs through the same four-phase structure — so you always know what we're doing and why.

01

Discovery & scoping

We map your technical environment, constraints, and goals before touching a codebase. Deliverable: a scoped architecture brief.

02

System design & prototype

Architecture diagrams, data models, and interactive prototypes for sign-off. Nothing gets engineered until we agree on structure.

03

Phased build & testing

Milestone-based delivery with continuous integration, automated test suites, and weekly client reviews built in.

04

Launch & knowledge transfer

Full documentation, runbooks, and a handoff session so your team owns the system — not just the surface.
